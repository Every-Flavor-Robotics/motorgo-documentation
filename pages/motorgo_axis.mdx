# MotorGo Axis


## Key Features

- **6–15V Barrel Jack** – ⚠️ Center-positive!
- **2x Brushless Motor Drivers** – Supports up to **10A per channel**, designed for **SimpleFOC**.
- **2x Encoder Ports** – Plug-and-play compatibility with **EncoderGo**.
- **9-DOF IMU** – **LSM6DSO** accelerometer and **LIS3MDL** magnetometer.
- **2x Qwiic Ports** – **4-pin I2C connectors** for sensors and peripherals.
- **GPIO Header** – Refer to the **pin diagram below** for details.
- **USB-C Port** – Program Plink over a **USB-C connection**.

## Setup
The MotorGo Axis supports two modes to fit your project needs. In Standalone Mode, it acts as the main controller, requiring no additional compute hardware. In PiHat Mode, it offloads motor control to the Plink while a Raspberry Pi handles the rest of the high-level processing to provide more flexibility.
