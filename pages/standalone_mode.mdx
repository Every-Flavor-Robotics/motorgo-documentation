The MotorGo can operate as a standalone microcontroller and motor controller combo. For most robots, this mean that the MotorGo can handle all of the compute needed to fully control the robot.

The onboard ESP32 can be programmed with the Arduino programming language in Arduino IDE or PlatformIO.
